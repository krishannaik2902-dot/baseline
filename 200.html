§ 01How it works

Twenty-nine blunt questions. One honest number.

You answer the questions a sharp GP would ask if they had more than nine minutes. How you actually sleep. How often you get out of breath. Who you could call at 2am. A rules engine weighs your answers by strength of evidence and scores eight domains. Sleep and movement count double, because in the research, they do.

Week one of your plan is five small changes, not a life overhaul. Retake the assessment in eight weeks and try to beat your number. That’s the whole product.

§ 02Where the advice comes from

Named sources. Graded evidence. No secret sauce.

Every recommendation shows the rule that fired, the strength of the evidence behind it, and where it comes from — Matthew Walker on sleep, Andy Galpin and Peter Attia’s Outlive on training, Jari Laukkanen’s cohort studies on sauna, Susanna Søberg on cold, Julianne Holt-Lunstad’s meta-analyses on social connection. The famous protocols — Blueprint, Huberman Lab — appear where they agree with the underlying research, and get flagged where they run ahead of it.

Three grades, honestly applied: Strong means multiple good human studies. Moderate means decent evidence with caveats. Emerging / debated means interesting, unproven, and we say so.

§ 03What we won’t tell you

The skeptic’s page is part of the product.

Continuous glucose monitors for healthy people. NMN. Hydrogen water. Greens powders. Most of what the cold-plunge industry claims. We keep a running page of popular things we don’t recommend and exactly why — because a plan you can trust has to be willing to say “the data isn’t there yet.” We sell no supplements and take no affiliate money, so we have no reason to pretend otherwise.

§ 04Your data

We can’t sell what we never see.

Your answers are stored in your browser’s local storage and nowhere else. There is no server to breach, no account to hack, no analytics script watching you scroll, and no cookie banner because there are no cookies. If you clear your browser data, your plan is gone — that’s the deal, and we think it’s a good one. The whole privacy policy is four paragraphs.

§ 05Free and Pro

The free plan is a real plan.

Free gets you the full assessment, your scored baseline, the safety checks, a first week of changes, and the top recommendations for every domain — with sources. Pro adds the full protocol library, a 12-week progression that adapts when you re-test, printable tracking sheets, and the self-test benchmarks (grip, dead-hang, step test). One-time unlock, or a cheap year — no forever-subscription.

The part our lawyer likes: Baseline is educational information, not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. No doctor–patient relationship is created by using it. Talk to a healthcare professional before changing your diet, exercise, supplements, or medication — especially if you’re pregnant, on medication, or have a health condition. If something feels urgent, it probably is: call your doctor, not a website.
