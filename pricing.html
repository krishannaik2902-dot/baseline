Answers to fair questions

Why one payment instead of a subscription?

Because a plan you follow for twelve weeks shouldn’t bill you for thirty-six months. If we add enough new value later to justify more money, we’ll ask for it then, transparently. (An optional supporter subscription may come later for people who want to fund the project — it will never be required.)

What’s never paywalled?

Anything safety-related: the flags, the see-a-doctor notices, the crisis resources. Also the assessment itself, your scores, and the ability to retake it. We think charging for “find out if you should see a doctor” would be grubby.

Refunds?

14 days, no questions asked, email and it’s done. UK/EU statutory withdrawal rights apply on top — see the Terms.

Do you make money any other way?

No. No supplement sales, no affiliate links, no ads, no data — we can’t sell what we never see. Pro is the entire business model, which is exactly why the recommendations don’t have to flatter any product.
